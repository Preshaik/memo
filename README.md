Goal: Support the Development of a Health and Well-being Resource Library

By the end of Q2, I will support the development of a Health and Well-being Resource Library by collaborating with the Health and Well-being Committee Chair, HR, and Public Affairs to research, source, and organize high-quality articles, tools, and resources that promote employee wellness.
