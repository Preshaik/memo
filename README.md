To help protect against phishing, please use this quick checklist whenever you receive an approval email:

1) Check the sender email address
A legitimate notification will come from noreply@intacct.com (not just a display name that says “Intacct”). Click the sender name to confirm the full address.

2) Check the spelling and format
The name should be spelled Intacct (I-N-T-A-C-C-T). These emails are typically system-generated and often include “Do not reply to this email.”

3) Look for real transaction details
Legit approval emails usually include specific information such as:

* requester name
* document number
* submission date
* vendor
* amount

4) Hover over the link (don’t click yet)
Hover your mouse over the “view/approve” link and check the preview URL. It should point to your normal Intacct sign-in/tenant address. If it looks unfamiliar, don’t click.

5) When in doubt, don’t click — log in directly ✅
If you’re unsure, open Sage Intacct from your saved bookmark or the Finance training site and approve from inside Intacct (Approvals/Notifications). This is the safest option.

If anything looks unusual, please pause and contact the Finance/IT support team.
