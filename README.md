
Over the past few months, you’ve heard updates during our All Staff meetings about the ERP initiative led by the Finance team.

We’re excited to share that Sage Intacct Phase 1 will go live on April 1, 2026 🎉

This marks the beginning of a new way of managing purchasing, approvals, and financial processes at FMB.

⸻

🔍 What’s Happening (and When)
	•	Go-Live Date: April 1, 2026
	•	What’s Launching: Phase 1 of Sage Intacct, focused on purchasing and approvals
	•	What’s Coming Next: Additional enhancements, including reporting improvements and a new expense system (Ramp) in upcoming phases

⸻

🧭 What This Means for You

This phase is primarily focused on Finance teams and staff involved in purchasing and approvals.
	•	If your role is directly involved, you’ll receive targeted communications, training, and support ahead of go-live
	•	If not, no immediate action is required at this time

⸻

🌟 Why This Matters

This change will help:
	•	Simplify and modernize our processes
	•	Improve visibility and tracking
	•	Strengthen financial controls

⸻

👀 What to Expect Next

Over the coming weeks, you’ll continue to see:
	•	Updates and reminders
	•	Training sessions (for impacted users)
	•	Helpful resources and guides

⸻

This is just the first step in our broader ERP journey, with more enhancements — including Ramp for expense management — coming soon.

Thank you for your continued support as we move forward.
